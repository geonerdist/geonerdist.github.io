About

Senior GIS Administrator / Search & Rescue Volunteer

Curtis is an experienced Geographic Information System Professional with a demonstrated history of working in the environmental services and AEC industry. Skilled in managing enterprise ArcGIS infrastructure, database administration, building field data collection workflows, developing geoprocessing scripts and tools, and delivering high-quality spatial data products for engineering and environmental clients.

Curtis holds a Bachelor of Science in Earth Sciences and a Master of Arts in Homeland Security with a concentration in Emergency Management & Geographic Information Technology from Northeastern University. Also serves as Communications Team Manager at Gallatin County Sheriff Search & Rescue, applying GIS skills in real-world life-safety operations.

Recognized with the GIS Professional Livability Award for contributions to the Montana GIS community in 2024.
